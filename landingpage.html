FRESHSENSE

The New Way to Protect Mushroom Farms Using the Power of Smell

In the world of farming, mushrooms are special. They grow fast, they are healthy, and they are becoming more popular around the world. But mushroom growing also comes with a big problem: they are very sensitive. A small change in their environment can cause mold, bad bacteria, or unwanted smells that can ruin the entire harvest. For many farmers, this means losing money, wasting time, and feeling stressed every growing cycle.

This is where FreshSense comes in—a new startup using smell-detection technology paired with smart software to help mushroom farmers protect their crops before anything goes wrong. FreshSense turns the natural power of smell into a tool that keeps farms safer, cleaner, and more profitable.

How FreshSense works

Smart Odor Sensor

This is the small device placed in mushroom growing rooms. It checks the air for certain smells that are known to be early signs of trouble. It doesn’t need water, sunlight, or complicated setup—just place it and turn it on.

Mobile App for Farmers

The app shows what FreshSense is detecting. If the air is clean and healthy, everything will appear green. If something unusual starts to happen, the app changes color and sends a message like:

  • "Warning: Possible mold activity in Room 2."
  • "Alert: Air quality changing-check humidity or ventilation."

Farmers can then act quickly by adjusting temperature, cleaning a tray, improving airflow, or removing infected spots.
Everything is designed to be easy to understand so any farmer, no matter their background, can use it comfortably.
